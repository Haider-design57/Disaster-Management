Mental Health & Coping

Disasters can affect the mind as much as the body. Stay strong mentally to recover faster.

How to Stay Calm

  • Take deep breaths, avoid panic
  • Talk to family, friends, or support groups
  • Focus on what you can control, not on fear

How to Handle Stress After Disaster

  • Accept that feeling anxious is normal
  • Help others → it reduces your own stress
  • Take breaks from news if it increases panic
  • Sleep and eat properly to recover
  • If feeling overwhelmed → seek helpline support
